# Rawlabs UI Design Skill

## Brand Feel
Rawlabs should feel:
- Premium
- Clean
- Trustworthy
- Natural
- Modern
- Pet-friendly

The design should not feel cheap, crowded, childish, or overly cartoonish.

## Brand Colors
Use Rawlabs brand colors as the main visual direction:
- Fuchsia
- Purple
- Soft neutral backgrounds
- White space

Do not randomly introduce unrelated dominant colors.

## Visual Style
Use realistic, high-quality visuals.
Prefer real-looking cats and dogs.
Avoid anime, cartoon, childish, or exaggerated styles unless explicitly requested.

Product images must look clean and premium.
Treat product cards as a key conversion area.

## Layout Principles
Use clean spacing.
Avoid clutter.
Keep sections visually balanced.
Use clear hierarchy:
- Strong headline
- Short explanation
- Clear call-to-action
- Trust elements

## Mobile Design Rules
Mobile view is critical.

Always check:
- No horizontal overflow
- Buttons are easy to tap
- Product cards stack properly
- Text is readable
- Header/navigation remains usable
- Cart and CTA buttons remain visible

## Product Card Rules
Product cards should include:
- Clear product image
- Product name
- Product type/category
- Price
- Clear CTA button
- Link to product detail

Do not make product cards too crowded.

## E-commerce Trust Elements
Where relevant, include trust-focused elements such as:
- Secure payment
- Fast shipping
- WhatsApp support
- Natural ingredients
- Freeze-dried production
- No additives

Only use claims that are true or can be verified.

## Avoid
Avoid excessive animations.
Avoid too many gradients.
Avoid large image files.
Avoid low contrast text.
Avoid visual changes that damage product readability.
